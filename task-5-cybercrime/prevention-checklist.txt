PREVENTION CHECKLIST 
Stay Safe Online
•	Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details 
•	Avoid clicking on suspicious links 
•	Always check website URLs carefully 
•	Use strong and unique passwords 
•	Enable two-factor authentication 
•	Do not trust unknown emails/messages 
•	Regularly update your apps and devices 
•	Avoid using public WiFi for transactions 
•	Verify messages from banks or institutions 
UPI/Financial Safety:
•	Always double-check UPI IDs before sending money 
•	Do not approve unknown payment requests 
Report Cybercrime:
•	National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in 
•	Helpline: 1930
