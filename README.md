##  Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course. The aim of this project is to develop essential digital skills, including creating a professional online presence, understanding safe internet practices, using collaboration tools, and learning proper digital communication.

The project consists of five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy. All tasks are organized in this repository along with relevant screenshots, documents, and reflections.

---

##  Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. The infographic covers topics such as safe internet practices, email etiquette, and the importance of digital skills for students.

---

##  Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and ResearchGate to build a professional digital presence. These platforms will help me showcase my skills, connect with professionals, and explore academic and career opportunities.

---

##  Task 3 – Platforms
I explored coding and collaboration tools by solving a beginner-level problem on HackerRank and creating a Google Form titled “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz.” This task helped me understand how online platforms support learning and data collection.

##  Task 4 – Email Etiquette
I drafted two professional emails: one requesting an assignment extension and another applying for an internship. I also created a checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media usage.

---

##  Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
I studied phishing as a form of cybercrime and created a case study explaining how such attacks occur. I also prepared a prevention checklist with safety tips and reporting methods to promote awareness among students.
